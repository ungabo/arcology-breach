# Steam Lighting Fixture Set 12 Catalog

Version: `0.1.57-p012`

- `SLF12_PREFAB_01_WallGaslightCage` - wall gaslight cage; 43 renderers. Wall-backed caged gaslight with layered brass frame, warm glass core, blackened iron guard rods, rivets, pipe feed, and soot halo card.
- `SLF12_PREFAB_02_HangingLantern` - hanging lantern; 35 renderers. Ceiling-hung brass lantern with chain links, canopy, glass barrel, cage rings, guard rods, caps, steam vent collar, and soot shading.
- `SLF12_PREFAB_03_VerticalInspectionLamp` - vertical inspection lamp; 41 renderers. Tall wall inspection lamp with amber tube glass, riveted iron rails, brass wall plate, copper feed pipe, grime cards, and service fasteners.
- `SLF12_PREFAB_04_PressureGaugeLampMount` - pressure gauge lamp mount; 53 renderers. Combined gauge and lamp bracket with readable pressure dial, needle, ticks, lamp capsule, pipe yoke, rivets, and soot bloom.
- `SLF12_PREFAB_05_PipeBracketGlowGlass` - pipe bracket with glow glass; 22 renderers. Horizontal pipe bracket with glowing inset glass, brass clamp collars, blackened iron pipe, wet reflective streaks, rivets, and porcelain insulators.
- `SLF12_PREFAB_06_BrassSwitchBox` - brass switch box; 34 renderers. Riveted brass wall switch box with lever, red enamel lockout slot, porcelain terminals, pilot glass, cable glands, soot, and oil staining.
- `SLF12_PREFAB_07_SootHaloDecalPlane` - soot halo decal plane; 2 renderers. Transparent soot and grime decal plane for lamp smoke halos behind fixtures.
- `SLF12_PREFAB_08_FixtureFamilyLineup` - fixture family lineup; 230 renderers. Palette prefab collecting the seven core Set12 fixture assets for quick isolated review.

Limitations: procedural geometry and textures are lookdev-ready but not final hand-authored sculpts; no gameplay colliders, no authored LOD groups, no scene integration, and no real-time light components are embedded in the prefabs.
