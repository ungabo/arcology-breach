# SBD12 QA Summary

SurfaceBreakupDecalSet12 is visual-only corridor dressing. The pack has no gameplay scripts, colliders, lights, scenes, audio, external DCC files, or custom shaders.

## Quick Checks

- Required decal families are covered: soot streaks, wet oil trails, damp puddle strips, warm light stains, pipe leak grime, brass edge wear, chipped wall grime, floor scuffs, corner darkness, and rivet rust halos.
- Albedo maps carry transparent alpha breakup; mask maps pack metallic, occlusion, alpha, and smoothness channels for Standard fallback or URP-compatible reuse.
- Prefabs are single built-in Quad cards with shadows and probes disabled.
- Concept renders include a contact sheet, corridor application demo, low-angle wet floor demo, and alpha/mask read.
