# Steampunk North Star

## Purpose

This document anchors the visual and thematic target for `Brassworks Breach`.

The project should lean heavily steampunk: brass, copper, soot, riveted iron, pressure gauges, valve wheels, gear locks, steam vents, gaslight, clockwork automata, furnace glow, oil-dark stone, and Victorian industrial machinery. A small amount of retro-futurist crossover is acceptable only when it still feels handmade, pressure-driven, mechanical, and analog.

## What To Build Toward

- Compact dungeon corridors with strong industrial function.
- Riveted iron wall panels, soot brick, wet stone, copper pipe bundles, steam vents, and gear-driven doors.
- Clockwork enemies with piston limbs and furnace-like eyes.
- A brass-and-walnut pressure pistol with readable gauge/ammo feedback.
- A HUD that feels like a small mechanical instrument panel.
- Props that communicate rules: keys look like gear keys, locks look like pressure locks, exits look like service lifts.

## What To Avoid

- Bright futuristic city-light styling.
- Floating digital display styling.
- Black chrome.
- Corporate sci-fi signage.
- Purely electronic weapons.
- Smooth futuristic robots.
- Clean sci-fi corridors.
