# V0.1.55 Pipe Tank Gauge Set 10 Asset Production Notes

Unity-only sidecar production pass focused on corridor machinery material readability.

## North-Star Material Read

- Aged brass and polished brass edgewear are reserved for brackets, bezels, collars, caps, and screw highlights.
- Heat-stained copper owns the pipe-run silhouettes and elbow language.
- Blackened and oily black iron define support mass, wall plates, yokes, hangers, and nozzle housings.
- Ivory dials, amber glass, and red enamel are used sparingly as focal points.
