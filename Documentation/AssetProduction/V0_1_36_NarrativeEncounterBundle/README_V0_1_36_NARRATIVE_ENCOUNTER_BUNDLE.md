## Handoff Summary

The bundle provides:

- A five-level room-by-room narrative and encounter beat sheet.
- Enemy introduction context for Scrapper, Lancer, Bellows Node, Bulwark, and Governor Warden.
- Objective signage, lore plate, secret-language, and setpiece hook candidates.
- Grouped reusable asset families suitable for production planning.
- Integration notes that preserve current route authority.
- Windows performance, Android/WebGL, and VR compatibility guidance.
- Acceptance gates and targeted validation ideas for future integration.

## Top Recommendations

1. Integrate narrative/setpiece dressing as visual children of existing authoritative route objects whenever possible.
2. Start with objective signage and state color consistency before adding ambient machinery.
3. Treat Level03, Level04, and Level05 as readability risk zones because they already combine hazards, heavier enemies, VFX, and objective state changes.
4. Build signage, worker marks, plaques, and secret cache dressing as reusable atlased families rather than bespoke room props.
5. Keep every lore beat optional, short, and physically grounded in the Brassworks.
