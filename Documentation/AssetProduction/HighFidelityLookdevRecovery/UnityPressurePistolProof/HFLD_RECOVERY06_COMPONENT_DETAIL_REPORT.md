## Honest Visual Read

Recovery06 is a useful component-first improvement, not final art. The coil, gauge, barrel/tank, and muzzle now carry more of the north-star brassworks language through layered collars, rivets, dark iron, copper/brass variation, and warmer edge hits. The weakest areas are still the grip/hand, which remains an assembled primitive placeholder rather than a sculpted gloved hand, and the overall material response, which is still Unity primitive lookdev rather than authored high-fidelity metal, glass, leather, and wood. The bottom-row renders also retain some empty black around the isolated components, though less than the prior sheet.
