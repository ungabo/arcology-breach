# V0.1.55 Pressure Pistol Hero Set 10 Asset Production Notes

Package: com.brassworks.sidecar.pressure-pistol-hero-set10
Folder: AssetPacks/BrassworksBreach.PressurePistolHeroSet10

## Scope

Component-first pressure pistol hero weapon package covering coil pack, pressure gauge, barrel/tank, muzzle, wood/leather grip, fasteners, and one candidate assembly. Components are designed to be independently reviewable before full assembly approval.

## Counts

- Prefabs: 7
- Materials: 8
- Textures: 13
- Reusable meshes: 9
- Preview PNGs including contact sheet: 8

## Safety

Visual-only. No colliders, rigidbodies, cameras, scene files, audio, animator controllers, gameplay scripts, Blender files, or external DCC source files are included.
