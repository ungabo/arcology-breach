# Import / Promotion Recommendation

Recommendation: **Revise**

Do not import the generated Unity project or blockout geometry into the main Brassworks Breach manifest. The pass succeeds as quarantined visual lookdev and should influence final weapon art direction, but it is not production-ready asset content.

Promote these decisions into final product work:

- Aged brass trim against a dark iron receiver.
- Exposed heat-stained copper coil as a readable side/lower silhouette feature.
- Amber pressure glass tube as a top-line identity marker.
- Gauge/dial, valve, rivets, and grime as required readability details.
- Muzzle crown/port cluster as a stronger weapon-family signature.

Revise before production import:

- Replace primitive blockout with authored meshes and bevels.
- Author proper PBR texture sets and grime/edge-wear masks.
- Rebuild the gauge and glass shader treatment for final readability.
- Validate first-person placement against the actual weapon rig and camera.
